About

My name is Kevin Jaworski. I look for new ways to solve new problems.

I attend Trine University in Angola, IN, where I am pursuing three different degrees. My latest degree is a Master of Science in Business Analytics. Through this degree program, I have gained comopetency in basic programming skills for Python, R, and SQL, as well as a deep understanding of process improvement. In this degree path, I have proven my understanding of finance, statistics, and digital marketing, alongside a strong understanding of project management. For the summer of 2020, I gained a great deal of knowledge through an internship in the process improvement department at MetroNet, a fiber optic service provider based in Evansville, IN.
